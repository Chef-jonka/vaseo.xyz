Chapter 01

The Problem

Here's the thing. AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity are now part of how people find information. They send bots to crawl websites, just like Google does.

But most website owners have no idea if these bots are actually reaching their content. Are they getting blocked? Are they hitting errors? Is your site even accessible to AI?

If AI bots can't crawl your site properly, your content won't show up in AI-generated answers. Simple as that. And you'd never know there was a problem in the first place.

Chapter 04

What I Need From You

Just one thing, really. Your server logs.
  • 01 Server access logs - These are the raw logs from your web server (Apache, Nginx, or similar). They contain every request made to your site, including bot visits.
  • 02 At least a few days of data - The more data, the better the insights. A week or a month gives a clearer picture of bot behaviour.
  • 03 That's it - I handle the rest. Parsing, analysis, and generating your report.

Not sure how to get your server logs? No problem. I can walk you through it, or work with your developer to get what's needed.
