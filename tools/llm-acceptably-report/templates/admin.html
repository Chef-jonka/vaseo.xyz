How to Generate Reports

1. Create a client account using the form above.

2. Click "Upload Log File" button to upload their server log.

3. Select the client and upload their Apache/Nginx access log file.

4. The system will analyze the log for AI bot traffic and generate a report.

5. Share the login credentials with your client so they can view their reports.
