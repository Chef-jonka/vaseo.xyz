Chapter 03

The Services

Every business is different. I offer SEO services that focus on clarity, structure, and long-term organic growth.

01

Audit

A full review of your website to understand how it performs in search. I look for technical issues, content gaps, and missed opportunities that limit visibility and growth.

02

Strategy & Roadmaps

A clear SEO plan built around your business goals. I define priorities, set direction, and create a roadmap that shows what to work on and why.

03

Technical SEO

Work focused on how search engines access and understand your site. This includes structure, speed, indexing, and fixing issues that block performance.

04

Content Strategy

Planning and shaping content based on what people actually search for. The goal is to create pages that rank, answer questions, and support business outcomes.

05

Visibility on LLMs

An overview of how your brand appears in large language models and AI-driven search tools. I assess whether and how your content is surfaced, referenced, or missed, and what can be improved to increase visibility.
